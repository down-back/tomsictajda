I was born in 1996 and for most of my life, I have lived in Slovenia. In February 2020, I obtained my Bachelor’s degree in Sculpture at the Academy of Fine Art and Design in Ljubljana. In 2018/19 I had a residency at the Academy of Fine Arts in Warsaw, Poland within the Erasmus+ program. In 2022 I obtained my Master’s in Fine Art at the University of the Arts in Zürich - ZHdK and was granted The Swiss Goverment Excellence Scholarship for foreign students, during my studies.
